# Favorites Selection Inconsistency

**Description:**  
When adding multiple products to favorites quickly (e.g., 5 in a row), some items are automatically deselected. 
Adding items individually via the product page works correctly.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Open BurgerKing app.  
2. Navigate to a product list.  
3. Quickly add 5 products to favorites in succession.  
4. Observe some products get deselected.  
5. Open each product individually and add to favorites one by one.  
6. Confirm all products are added successfully.

**Expected Result:**  
All selected products should be added to favorites regardless of speed.

**Actual Result:**  
Rapid multi-selection causes some items to deselect; only individual selection works.

**Severity / Priority:**  
- Severity: Medium  
- Priority: Medium
